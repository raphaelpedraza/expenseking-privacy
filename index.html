Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Thank you for using ExpenseKing. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the application.

1. Information You Enter in the App

ExpenseKing allows you to record and manage personal financial information such as:

  • Expenses
  • Categories
  • Wallet balances
  • Transaction notes
  • App preferences

This information is not collected by the developer and is not transmitted to any external servers.

All data you enter into the app is stored locally on your device only. The developer of ExpenseKing does not have access to your financial data.

If you choose to export your data (for example via CSV export or sharing features), the data will only be shared through your device's built-in sharing tools and only when you explicitly initiate that action.

2. Local Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage (SQLite database). This means:

  • Your financial data remains on your device
  • No backend servers store your information
  • No user accounts or authentication are required
  • The developer cannot access your stored data

If you uninstall the application, your stored data may also be removed depending on your device's operating system.

3. Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications to remind you to log expenses.

These reminders are scheduled entirely on your device using local notification features. No notification data is sent to external servers and no remote push notification service is used.

Notification scheduling information remains stored locally on your device.

4. Advertising

ExpenseKing displays advertisements provided by Google through Google AdMob.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information to provide and improve advertising services. This may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Approximate location information
  • Interaction with advertisements

This information is collected directly by Google and is governed by Google's own privacy policies.

The app is configured to request non-personalized advertisements only. This means AdMob serves ads without using behavioral or interest-based targeting tied to your profile.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can also learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services here:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob (for displaying advertisements)

No analytics services, crash reporting tools, or other third-party tracking SDKs are used in the app.

6. Permissions

The app may request the following device permission:

Notifications – Used to schedule optional spending reminder notifications that help you remember to log expenses.

Notification permissions are optional and can be disabled at any time through your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

ExpenseKing is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to address the situation.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on the application's listing page.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

9. Revoking Consent

If you previously consented to personalized advertising and wish to withdraw that consent, you can do so at any time by:

  • Resetting your device's Advertising ID — go to your device Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset Advertising ID
  • Uninstalling the application
  • Contacting us at expenseking2026@gmail.com and we will assist you

Revoking consent means you will continue to see ads, but they will be non-personalized.
